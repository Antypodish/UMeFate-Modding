# UMeFate-Modding
This repository refers to modding files, like Lua, json, etc.

## License
***
1. Enforced license is GPL-3.0.
2. In short this means, that files based on UMeFate modding repository, as well, as sourced directly from UMeFate software of its modding directory, must stay under its original license.
3. Files and forked repositories can be redistributed freely, but must stay open-source. This is for the modding transparency and ensuring for a helathy modding ecosystem.

4. Furthermore, withouth UMeFate owner's written permission the original, or derivative work **MAY NOT**:
   
    a) be closed-source,
  
    b) be made as an independent comercial product, or be part of it,
  
    c) be paywalling of any form (having limited access to mod(s)),
  
    d) contain, or reference any form of micro transactions.
  
6. Modders of their work **CAN** however, monnetise their mods via crowd founding. For an example via social media, patreon etc.
7. If any file comes from 3rd party source, licence must be provided.
8. Mods, however **doesn't require** providing source files to audio, or images. But such files **must** provide their respective license.
9. If license is not provided within the mod, at the request, modder must be able to provide prove of their work, or present the permission to used assets within a mod. This requirement is to prevent breaking related mods, in case of any DMCA (take down notice) that may be issued to the mod.
10. If modder uses other mods as a dependency, to build their own mods, modders are responsible to ensure, any asset has permission to be used within modding enviroment.
11. No mod is permitted to ripp, or reuse other mod's assets, withouth that mod's owner permission. To reference other mods assets, such dependant mod can use dependancy worklfow, by link relevant assets within UMeFate modding directory.
12. Modders are responsible to provide prove of their work, in case of any dispute.
